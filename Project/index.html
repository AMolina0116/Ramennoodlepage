How to make Ramen Noodles

For Regular Noodles

To make the regular chicken noodles follow the steps below

  1. Grab the noodle bowl out of the cupboard
  2. Take your package of noodles and in the package and break
    it in half then break the top and bottom in half again
  3. Pour them into the noodle bowl and take out the seasoning packet
  4. Fill the noodle bowl up to the line with hot water
  5. Microwave the noodles for 3 minutes
  6. Take the noodles out of the microwave and break up all the noodles
    by mixing the cooked noodles up with your fork
  7. Take the noodle bowl to the sink and carefully tip the noodle bowl and
    with your fork hold the noodles back and strain most of the water out
  8. Open the seasoning packet pour it in the noodles and stir completely
  9. Enjoy!
